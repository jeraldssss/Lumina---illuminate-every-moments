How we work

"At Lumina, we ensure every event is seamless, memorable, and tailored to your vision. Here’s how we bring your celebrations to life:

1. Consultation & Idea Sharing We start by understanding your vision, preferences, and goals for the event. Every detail matters—from theme, colors, and style to the atmosphere you want to create.

2. Planning & Proposal Our team creates a detailed plan including design concepts, budget, timeline, and logistics. You’ll get a clear proposal to review and approve before we move forward.

3. Design & Preparation We bring the vision to life with décor, lighting, seating arrangements, and all the creative elements. We coordinate vendors, materials, and schedules to ensure everything is ready.

4. Event Setup & Execution On the big day, we manage the setup, decorations, and flow of the event. Our team ensures everything runs smoothly while you focus on enjoying the celebration.

5. Post-Event Wrap-Up We handle teardown, cleanup, and any final touches. We also gather feedback to continually improve and ensure every client’s experience is perfect.

At Lumina, every step is designed to make your event shine—from the first idea to the last spark.
